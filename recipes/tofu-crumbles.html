Crispy Tofu

Home

Description

Delicious crispiness which can be used as a topping for pasta or filling for tacos.
It is an easy way to boost a dish with some protein.

Ingredients

  • 200 gm Firm Tofu
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Paste/Ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Red chilli sauce(optional)
  • 1 tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • Seasonings: Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Red Chilli Powder/Flakes, Ground Black Pepper, Smoked Paprika, Peri-Peri (Start with less amount and adjust as needed)

Steps

  1. Take out tofu from its package, drain the excess water and pat it dry.
  2. In a bowl, mix all the ingredients along with crumbles tofu, you can also shred the tofu.
  3. Let it marinade for around 30 minutes or overnight in the refrigerator.
  4. Add the tofu along with the marinade on a air fryer friendly container/liner and spread it evenly.
  5. Put it in the air frier for 10-15 minutes at 170 C and keep shaking the container every 5 minutes.
  6. And the tofu crumbles are ready to eat! Serve it on pasta or use it to make a delicious taco filling.
